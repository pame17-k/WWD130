Betru Company

Company Purpose Provide thrilling and safe rafting experiences, connecting people with nature and promoting responsible adventure. Mission Deliver unforgettable adventures on the best rivers, with expert guides and a strong commitment to safety, environmental respect, and fun. Creed We believe in the thrill of moving waters, the unity of teamwork, the respect for nature, and the importance of adventure to enrich the soul. Slogan "Adventure awaits!" or a more dynamic option like "Ride the current, feel the thrill."

History

Betru Company was born out of a passion for adventure and the desire to share the thrill of rafting with people from all over the world. Founded by a group of extreme sports enthusiasts, the company began as a small team of experienced guides exploring the most challenging rivers.
